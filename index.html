Cam Thomas’s Early-Career Knack for Scoring is Unprecedented Among his Peers

Thomas is becoming an explosive scorer quickly into his NBA career. Quicker, the numbers show, than any of the other top scorers in today’s NBA.

By CARLOS GARCIA

November 30, 2023

On November 6th against the Milwaukee Bucks, Brooklyn Nets Guard Cam Thomas scored 45 points. It was the fifth time in his career he scored 40 or more points in a game. The 22 year-old had played just 2,363 career minutes before achieving this feat.

So far this season, Cam Thomas ranks 12th in points per game (26.9) among players who have played in at least 8 games (stats as of Nov. 29). Among the 11 players ranked ahead of him, how many career minutes did they play before scoring at least 40 points in 5 different games?

“Cam Thomas is an exceptional scorer,” says Rob Mahoney, a Staff Writer who covers the NBA for The Ringer. “It’s not often that you’ll find players who are this explosive while managing to fly this far under the radar.”
